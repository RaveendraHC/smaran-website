About Us

Smaran Pyrolysis Technologies Pvt. Ltd. is a new-age sustainable renewable energy company, to produce 4th Generation Biofuels based on Agricultural wastes.
Providing complete range of technology solutions from waste Biomass aggregation to Catalytic Pyrolysis to Catalytic Refining of Bio Oil to ASTM grade Bio Diesel.
It started operations in 2021 with an experienced team of visionaries with the intent of providing affordable alternative green energy solutions using various biowaste.
It is a humble start to mitigate the unscientific burning of surplus agricultural waste.

Our team has researched and is continuously developing technologies as a solution to crop residue burning, Loss of valuable Carbon and Environmental pollution

Our research is based on the following scientific philosophies:
❖ Agri wastes can be Pyrolysed to Bio-Char and Bio-Fuel
❖ The need is to develop low-cost continuous pyrolysis and catalyst refining technologies to produce ASTM Grade Bio-Diesel
❖ Clean and Green Renewable Technology thus developed should be Scalable, Sustainable, Licensable

We are founded and managed by experienced professionals from Industry, Business, Academia, and research.

Our Mission

Renewable bio-mass circular economy

❖ Generation of circular Economy by supporting farmers in disposing of agricultural wastes scientifically as well as supporting them financially by purchasing from farm gate. Otherwise, the same bio-waste after harvesting is being burnt majorly contributing heavily to Greenhouse gasses.

Collection of Agri wastes

❖ Pursuing a robust green agenda in bio-fuel production by taking care to incorporate sustainability in the procurement as well as the production process.

Size Reduction for Pyrolysis

❖ Having a well-crafted blueprint for the technology developed which is continuous, optimized, and flexible along with being economical and scalable to assist in putting up decentralized facilities.

Continuous Catalytic Pyrolysis

❖ Investing time as well as resources in developing a win-win situation for all the channel partners in all areas of operation.

Catalytic Refining to Bio-Diesel

❖ All transactions are to be kept ethical, transparent, and fair. The information is to be available to both internal as well as external stakeholders.
