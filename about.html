About Us

Smaran Pyrolysis Technologies Pvt. Ltd. is a new-age sustainable renewable energy company operational in Tumkur. It started operations in 2021 with an experienced team of visionaries with the intent of providing affordable alternative green energy solutions using various biowaste materials. It is a humble start to mitigate the unscientific burning of surplus agricultural waste.
Our team has researched over several years and is continuously developing technologies as a solution to crop residue burning. Our research is based on the following scientific philosophies:

● Agri wastes can be Pyrolysed to Bio-Char and Bio-Fuel
● The development of low-cost continuous pyrolysis and catalyst refining technologies can lead to the production and refining of ASTM Grade Bio-Diesel

We are founded and managed by experienced professionals from Industry, Business, Academia, and research, the team is engaged in developing Scalable, Sustainable, Licensable, Clean, and Green Renewable Energy Technologies

Our Mission

● Generation of circular Economy by supporting the farmers in disposing of the bio-waste scientifically as well as supporting them financially by purchasing the bio-waste from them. The same bio-waste after harvesting is being burnt majorly contributing heavily to Greenhouse gasses.

● Pursuing a robust green agenda in bio-fuel production by taking care to incorporate sustainability in the procurement as well as the production process.

● Having a well-crafted blueprint for the technology developed which is continuous, optimized, and flexible along with being economical and scalable to assist in putting up decentralized facilities.

● Investing time as well as resources in developing a win-win situation for all the channel partners in all areas of operation.

● All transactions are to be kept ethical, transparent, and fair. The information is to be available to both internal as well as external stakeholders.

● Creating a sustainable Symbiotic Eco-System for Farmers – Investors – Industry – Country.
