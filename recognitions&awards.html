Recognitions, Awards and Certifications

Recognitions

  • As a company, it was recognised by Bio Energy Development & Research Development Center, Karnataka, and was awarded 'Innovative Start-Up Award' from Karantaka State Bio-Fuel Development Board

  • It has been recognized as a progressive start-up company by the Start-Up India seed fund in the field of alternative green energies

  • It has been recognized for the India start-up seed fund and procedures are in progress for a grant

  • As a recognized start-up by start-up India fund, company is eligible for upto Rs. 5 crore back ended subsidy after commissioning of the 4th generation bio-fuel plant from bio-mass pyrolysis.
