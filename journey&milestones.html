Journey and Milestones

OUR JOURNEY

Smaran, as a Company has been putting consistent efforts in research and development of Innovative and Sustainable Green Technology. For years now team SMARAN has been working on economical and scalable technologies to convert the abundantly available biomass into Bio-fuel and other bio-products.

Smaran aims at optimal utilization of surplus biomass which is currently being burnt in an unscientific way leading to enormous pollution and loss of valuable carbon economy. The pioneering vision of team SMARAN is to Convert Waste to Wealth. The vision is to make India self-reliant on Industrial and Transportation Fuels using Renewable Agricultural wastes and residues.



  • 2020

    Team SMARAN embarked on a Trans Indian Journey to understand the status, logistics, and economy of production for 1st generation Bio Diesel from Non-Edible Oils and concluded making Biodiesel from non-edible oil seeds is not a viable solution

  • 2021

    Team SMARAN along with consensus and consultation of specialists from the Petroleum Refining Industry, Professors in Fluid Dynamics, Chemistry Doctorates in Nano Particles, experts in heterogeneous catalysis, Professors in Mechanical Engineering, and Professional MBAs brainstormed and concluded that 4th Generation Bio Fuel Technology which uses and converts abundantly available Bio-mass into Bio Fuel is the way forward
    Team SMARAN decided to incubate at Siddaganga TBI, SIT Campus, and under the guidance of the Department of Chemical Engineering SIT, Tumkur carried out the required Laboratory trials to establish the Proof of Concept (POC). A 5 Liter/per day Pilot Plant was put up to validate the results

  • 2022

    Upon successful completion of Pilot trials SMARAN put up an Industrial Level Demonstration Unit at Bio Fuel Plant of SIT with a capacity of 1000 Liters per day and successfully demonstrated its Proprietary Engineering and Technological Skills

  • 2023

    ● Conducted 3 tonnes per batch (10 tonnes per day) pyrolysis oil conversion and refining to bio-diesel at an industrial scale using one of the allowance partner having 1st generation bio-fuel plant
    ● Establishment of Quality Excellence and Engine test of the bio-diesel for Industrial Scale production.
    ● R&D Collaboration with Department of Chemistry NIE, Mysuru and SJCE, Mysuru for Advanced Catalysis and Polymer Chemistry.

  • Journey 2024 onwards

    ● Scout for prospective investors and carry out investor roadshows for capital raising
    ● Establish 20 tonnes per day bio-fuel plant using strategic, venture capital and angel investor funds
    ● Work on further capacity expansion using internal approvals as per the capacity roadmap
    ● Develop licensable, scalable technologies for bio-phenols and bio-mulch products
